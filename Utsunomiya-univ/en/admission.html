■ Admission information

Application qualification
  • Graduate students in the Graduate School of Regional Development and Creativity at Utsunomiya University
  • Graduate students at partner universities
Enrollment capacity
  • 2 inbound students and 2 outbound students in FY2021:
    • 1 student from the University of Ghana
    • 1 student from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology
    • 2 students from Utsunomiya University
  • 5 students for exchange online program
Language

English

*When students from Africa come to Japan, they can take a beginner level Japanese class.

Financial support for study abroad The scholarship is planned to contains monthly stipend of 60,000 to 80,000 yen and support for travel expenses and study abroad insurance fee.

■ FAQs

  • Q. Is there any financial support to apply this program?

    A. The scholarship carries monthly stipend of 60,000 yen for students at Utsunomiya University who study in Africa, and 80,000 yen for students from Africa who study at Utsunomiya University. Also, support for travel expenses and study abroad insurance fee is planned.

  • Q. What is required to apply this program?

    A. You should belong to a graduate school at one of the universities listed below to apply this program:

    • Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology
    • Meru University of Science and Technology
    • Addis Ababa University
    • University of Dar es Salaam
    • Nelson Mandela African Institution of Science and Technology
    • University of Ghana

    In addition to the requirement mentioned above, you need to submit your grades and reasons for application for the screening. There is screening criteria for application.

  • Q. Should I learn languages other than English?

    A. Languages other than English is not necessary.
