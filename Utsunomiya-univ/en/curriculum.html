■ About Programs

Short term exchange program

Within 3 months

  • Summer program

    2 weeks (for first year students in the master’s course)

    During the summer vacation, 10 lectures, seminars, practical training, and experiments will be conducted. Also, a workshop will be held over the last two days, applying what have been learned.

    Students who participate in the workshop have the possibility of earning credits in "Practical Fieldwork" and "Practical Research".

  • Spring program

    2 weeks(for first year students in the master’s course)

    During the spring vacation, 10 lectures, seminars, practical training, and experiments will be conducted. Also, a workshop will be held over the last two days, applying what have been learned.

    Students who participate in the workshop have the possibility of earning credits in "Practical Fieldwork" and "Practical Research".

  • Short term internship program

    3 to 4 weeks (for second year students in the master’s course)

    International students from Africa will participate in the internship in Tochigi Prefecture and students from Japan will participate in it at Japanese companies in Africa.

Long term exchange program

1 year

Students from Africa will study at Utsunomiya University and students at Utsunomiya University will study at one of the partner universities in Africa. They both attend lectures and practical training at college.

Credits earned in this program will be recognized as credits required for graduation under an agreement on mutual recognition.
