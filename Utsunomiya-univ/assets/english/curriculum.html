Short term exchange program

Within 3 months

  • Summer program

    2 weeks

    During the summer vacation, 10 lectures, seminars, practical training, and experiments will be conducted . Also, a workshop will be held over the last two days.

    Students who participate in the workshop will receive credit for "Practical Fieldwork" and "Field Study.

  • Spring program

    2 weeks(Seminarian Program 1 year student)

    During the spring vacation period, 10 lectures will be given, and exercises, practical training, and experiments will be conducted for each program. In addition, a workshop will be held on the last two days of the program as a summary. Students who participate in the workshop will receive credit for "Practical Fieldwork" and "Field Research".

    Students who participate in the workshop will receive credit for "Practical Fieldwork" and "Field Research".

  • Short term internship program

    3 to 4 weeks(Seminarian Program 2 year student)

    The internships will be held in Tochigi prefecture for students from Africa and at Japanese companies in Africa for students from Japan. Students who participate in the internship will receive credits for "Practical Internship" and "International Internship".

Long term exchange program

1 year

Students will study abroad in Africa and at Utsunomiya University, and participate in lectures and practical training at the host university. The credits earned will be recognized as graduation credits through mutual recognition.
